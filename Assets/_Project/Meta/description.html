March Festival

Give your people peace, new elder!

About

March Festival is a cozy visual novel about partnership with your neighbours in a tiny medieval-styled fantasy village.

The previous elder is now just an Old Man. He hopes that the new elder (you) can bring much-needed peace and quiet to this village.

A nice and charming community dog called just Good Old Dog will follow you and help to connect with even the shyest people.
